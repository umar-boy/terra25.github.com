Technology and science

Since 2000, technology has accelerated: mobile networks, cloud services, and AI. Space science has gained new tools, such as telescopes and missions..

Future of Research (2026)

In 2026, active research on Mars is planned: robots and satellites will search for traces of life, analyze water and minerals, and scientists will test the feasibility of future manned missions..

Future of Research (2030)

By 2030, we can expect manned missions to the Moon and Mars, new telescopes for studying exoplanets, and advanced robotic missions to search for life and resources.

Colonization and Mars (2035)

In 2035, it is planned to build the first houses on Mars, use rockets and robots to transport resources. People will be able to fly to Mars for research, as well as study Earth from space and new technologies for living on other planets.
